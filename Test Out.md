## 20.3 Troubleshoot Mobile OS and App Security
- What are the security concerns associated with root access and jailbreaking on mobile devices?
	    The biggest security concern with Jailbreaking or Rooting a devices is its ability to now subvert security controls.  Giving the user admin access is never a good idea.
- How can Mobile Device Management (MDM) solutions detect and prevent access to rooted or jailbroken devices?
	- if can likely tell that the Phone has the ability to subvert security settings and MDM will kick it off of everything it knows to kick it off of and send info to IT.    
- What are the risks of installing apps from untrusted sources, and how can they be mitigated?
	    enabling sideloading weakens security.  It still allows possible nefarious apps to take advantage of the phone.
- What steps should you take to address unexpected application behavior that may indicate a security threat?
	    Disconnect Phone from network and isolate.
